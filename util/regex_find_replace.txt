HTML ids
--------------------
Find: (?<=id="[^"]*)-
Replace: _

Markdown Headers
--------------------
Find: ^(#{1,6})\s+\*\*(.*?)\*\*\s*$
Replace: $1 $2

Remove "strong" tag from Headers
--------------------------------
Find: (<h([1-6])\b[^>]*>)\s*<strong>(.*?)</strong>\s*</h\2>
Replace: $1$3</h$2>
